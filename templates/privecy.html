Relaxation Tools for Students

Effective Tools for Mental Health

Here are some relaxation tools to help manage stress and promote mental well-being:

  • Mindfulness and Meditation Apps:
    • Headspace
    • Calm
  • Breathing Exercises:
    • Breathe2Relax
    • Square Breathing Technique
  • Progressive Muscle Relaxation:
    • Audio Guides available on YouTube
  • Yoga and Stretching:
    • Yoga with Adriene (YouTube)
    • StretchIt App
  • Nature Sounds and Music:
    • Rain Rain (White Noise App)
    • Spotify (Create calming playlists)
  • Journaling:
    • Day One (Journaling App)
    • Expressive Writing (any notebook will do!)
  • Art and Creativity Tools:
    • Colorfy (Coloring App)
    • Canva (Creative Design Tool)
  • Physical Activity:
    • Check out YouTube for exercise videos
    • Join or form walking groups
  • Guided Visualization:
    • Relaxation Scripts available online
    • YouTube Guided Visualizations
  • Online Support Communities:
    • Discord (Join supportive groups)
    • 7 Cups (Online chat support)
